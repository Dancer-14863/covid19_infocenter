Sinovac COVID-19 vaccine or CoronaVac




Information about Sinovac Covid-19 Vaccine



Sinovac, also known as CoronaVac, is a covid-19 vaccine. It is manufactured by Sinovac Biotech Ltd. Its country of origin is China. The published price per dose of the Sinovac vaccine ranges from US$13.60 to US$29.75. So far, the total number of does ordered has reached 12 million. The number of doses required per person is 2 doses at intramuscular. The type of vaccine is a whole virus vaccine or an inactivated vaccine. The Sinovac vaccine's effectiveness in Turkey is 91.25%, Indonesia is 65-3%, and Brazil is 50.4%. The Challenge of this type of vaccine is booster shots may be required.

Benefits of Sinovac Vaccine

  • Its technology is well established.
  • It is suitable for those with weak or compromised immune system.
  • It is relatively simple to manufacture.
  • It can be stored in a standard refrigerator at 2-8 degrees Celsius, which means it is a lot more useful to developing countries which might not be able to store large amounts of vaccine at such low temperatures.
  • It has more advantages in the short run than the ones developed by the U.S Pfizer and Moderna as it uses traditional vaccine-making technology.
